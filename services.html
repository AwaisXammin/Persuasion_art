How we work

We start by developing a Thematic Calendar, featuring a chronological plan of quarterly Thought Leadership themes, each one custom tailored to match company priorities and product roadmap, ultimately leading to a clear and unique industry-leading position.

This process includes:

In depth interviews with key client executives across Sales, Product, Marketing, Business Development, and others to:

Unearth unique expertise and perspectives

Find and nurture a collectively shared vision for the future.

Extensive industry analysis to

Identify core elements of change

Choose underutilized perspectives and subjects where our client can claim ownership

Determine the most relevant industry events or channels to distribute our Thought Leadership

Research planning, to create a list of specific follow-on projects to support Themes:

Custom surveys

Quantitative analyses

In-the-wild product tests
